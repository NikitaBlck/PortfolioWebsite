SEAT MOUNT FOR SOLAR CAR - SOLARIDE

What?

  • Design as lightweight as possible mounting bracket for a car's seat
  • Test if it fails at 5G load beforethe manufacturing

How?

  • Sheet metal and composite parts designed in SolidWorks
  • FEA of the part (including compositestructures) in Ansys Workbench
  • Created a manufacturing drawing (bending)
  • Implemented DFA and DFM principles

Results

  • Part passed the FEA tests and was manufactured.
  • Has held for the entire race, seat was fixed tigtly to the frame with minimum overall weight
